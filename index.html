Sharing God's love through video games and community.

Video games can be a tool for sharing God's love, or as a way to relax at the end of the day.

That's why we created WDM GX. We created it as a way to encourage both while guiding people towards the best games for sharing God's love while respecting Him in what you play.

It can be difficult at the time of purchase to decide whether a game is appropriate for the Christian worldview.

That's why we are researching and reviewing games and giving them ratings on their moral tone, values, and more.
